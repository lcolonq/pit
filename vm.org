** what are the instructions?
- symbols look up their value binding and push it to the stack
- cons cells represent different operations
  + (cons 'quote v) for some value v pushes v to the stack
  + (cons 'apply a) for some arity a pops a+1 values from stack (funcalls top of stack symbol on the rest)
